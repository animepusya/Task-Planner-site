Support for Task Planner

Get help or send feedback

If you have questions, found a bug, or want to suggest an improvement, you can contact Task Planner support directly by email.

Contact support

The fastest way to get help is to send an email with a short description of the issue.

If possible, include your device model, iOS version, and a few details about what happened.

taskplanner.app@hotmail.com
Email support View Privacy Policy

What to include

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • A screenshot, if it helps explain the issue

Feedback

Suggestions for improving Task Planner are always welcome. If there is a feature you would like to see, feel free to write about it.
