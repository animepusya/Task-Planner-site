Task Planner Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

Task Planner is an iOS task management application designed to help you organize your tasks, schedule reminders, track progress, and view statistics. The app does not require account creation or sign-in.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. This may include:

  • tasks, notes, dates, reminders, categories and settings
  • small photo thumbnails attached to tasks
  • app preferences and widget data
  • calendar identifiers for sync

Task Planner does not upload your data to external servers.

Photos

Photos are optional. If you attach one, the app uses the iOS photo picker and only accesses the image you select. Photos are stored locally and are not uploaded.

Calendar

Calendar access is optional. If enabled, the app can create and sync tasks with Apple Calendar and display your events.

Notifications

Task Planner uses local notifications to remind you about tasks. No remote push notification servers are used.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, ads, or tracking services. It uses Apple frameworks such as StoreKit, EventKit, UserNotifications, and PhotosUI.

Data Sharing

Your data stays on your device. Task Planner does not sell, share, or track your data.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app.

Your Choices

  • You can disable photos, calendar, or notifications
  • You can edit or delete your data at any time
  • You can manage subscriptions via Apple
